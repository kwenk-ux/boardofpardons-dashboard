Understanding Dissenting Votes

The PA Board of Pardons has 5 members: the Lieutenant Governor (Chair), Attorney General, and three Governor-appointed members. A pardon recommendation requires a majority (3+ votes). This dataset only records dissenting votes — board members who voted differently from the majority. Members who always voted with the majority do not appear in this data. "Yes" dissenters voted FOR a pardon when the majority voted against. "No" dissenters voted AGAINST when the majority approved.
